How to Die Fast?

Step 1: Enroll in a Bachelor's program.

Step 2: Accept all deadlines without question. Time is an illusion. Everything is due tomorrow, even yesterday's homework.

Step 3: Convince yourself that starting your Bachelor’s thesis one day before the deadline is perfectly reasonable. After all, inspiration strikes at the last minute, right?

Step 4: Spend 80% of your writing time finding the “perfect font.” Because if your title doesn’t have personality, your paper won’t either.

Step 5: Procrastinate. Do anything other than your actual work. Write this story, for example.

Step 6: Realize that you’re 99% done… except for all the research, writing, and editing you haven’t actually done.

Step 7: Curl up in a ball and cry. Repeat daily.

Don’t worry, you're not alone. We're all slowly dying from deadlines too!
